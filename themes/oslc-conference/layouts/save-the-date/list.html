Save the Date!
The first conference on OSLC & LinkedData (OSLCFest) will take place in Stockholm on November 4th & 5th, 2018 at the KTH Royal Institute of Technology. OSLCFest will showcase the latest in technology to connect data across silos using standard APIs. The event will bring together experts in ALM/PLM/MBSE with experts in APIs, Linked Data, graph databases, and query languages.
 
 
 
Register Now!
 
 
 

Keynote Speakers
As keynote speakers, John Wiegand, former Chief Architect at Rational and one of the "fathers" of OSLC, as well as Mike Amundsen, internationally known author and Hypermedia API expert, as well as Prof. Soeren Auer, contributor to substantial semantic technologies such as DBPedia, will share their unique perspective on OSLC.
 
 
 
 
 
 

No Traditional Conference
OSLCFest will not be a traditional conference as we want to promote interactions among all participants. It will be an "unconference" with more panel discussions, more time for Q&A, and more time for everybody to voice their perspective. Please check out the preliminary agenda and please let us know if you want to participate!
 
 
Industry participants, for example from Airbus and Scania, will present their collaboration challenges. Vendors will give demos of their latest OSLC solutions. Contributors to OSLC standards and open-source will share their latest results. Members of the OSLC community will discuss OSLC evangelization strategies. A joint meeting between OSLC conference participants and the Nordic APIs meetup group will take place on the evening of the first day. Check out our Preliminary Agenda!
